Project 3:
Data Professional Survey Analysis

March 2024

Project Goal: Create a dashboard in Power BI to visualize data professionals' job satisfaction, salary by type of job, and demographics.

Introduction
The interactive dashboard I created for this project can be found here: Power BI File

Ask
How happy are data professionals with their job and how difficult was it to change careers into data?

Prepare
The survey data was collected from LinkedIn, twitter, and youtube community and finished with 630 respondents.

Process
I transformed the data in Power BI by deleting unnecessary columns, deleting duplicate entries, and changing column types to numerical to prepare for analysis.

Analyze and Share


Insights:

  1. Data scientist have the highest salary while database developers have the lowest
  2. Most respondents are data analysts, followed by data engineers and data scientists
  3. Most respondents did not start their career in data, and had moderate difficulty getting their first data job
  4. Respondents are slightly happy with their work-life balance and slightly unhappy with their salary

Insights:

  1. The most represented countries are The United States, India, and The United Kingdom
  2. Most respondents have a bachelors or masters degree
  3. Most respondents are caucasian or asian
  4. The average respondent age is 30 and the majority are male
